"Meet the brilliant minds taking the stage at TEDx IMI New Delhi 2025! This year's speakers are innovators, visionaries, and changemakers from diverse fields, each bringing a unique perspective and powerful story to share. Get ready to be inspired, challenged, and uplifted as they explore ideas that spark curiosity and drive transformation. Discover the voices shaping the future, one idea at a time."

Feel the magic of Purbayan Chatterjee, the visionary who bridges the timeless beauty of Indian classical music with modern innovation. From enchanting global audiences to creating chart-topping hits like "Garaj Garaj," his art defies boundaries and redefines possibility. Let his melodies inspire you to dream bigger.

Award-winning Sitar Maestro
Purbayan Chatterjee

Explore the extraordinary vision of Maj. Gen. Pramod Saighal, a trailblazer in education and disaster management whose impact spans millions. Honored with the prestigious Biju Patnaik Peace Award, his journey of hope and innovation will inspire you to shape a better tomorrow.

Visionary Leader & Changemaker
Maj. Gen. Pramod Saighal

Enter the world of storytelling mastery with Vijay S. Jodha, whose compelling creations have spanned 195 countries and over 250 festivals. With 100+ international accolades, including the coveted Peabody Award, his stories will take you beyond the ordinary, opening windows to realms unknown.

Acclaimed Filmmaker, Writer & Photographer
Vijay S. Jodha

Discover the groundbreaking innovations of Ajay Aggrawal, the visionary behind Humane Design and Innovation Consulting LLP. From revolutionizing packaging with eco-friendly solutions to pioneering human-centric design, his work is a blueprint for a greener, brighter future.

Innovative Pioneer in Sustainability
Ajay Aggrawal

Dive into the awe-inspiring feats of Shital Mahajan Rane, an adventurer who has redefined courage. A Padma Shri awardee with 8 world records, her skydiving exploits, from the icy wilderness of Antarctica to founding the Phoenix Skydiving Academy, will inspire you to defy limits and embrace fearlessness.

Extreme Sports Trailblazer & Record Holder
Shital Mahajan Rane

Step into the multifaceted world of Sugandha Vatrana, a Total Rewards leader with over 15 years of global expertise, yoga exponent, and spiritual guide. From uplifting millions through her Tata Play Fitness show "Yoga for Self Love" to leading the International Day of Yoga 2024 with PM Modi, she redefines balance and purpose in life.

Pioneer in Total Rewards & Yoga Advocate
Sugandha Vatrana
