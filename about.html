About TED

TED, which stands for "Technology, Entertainment, and Design," is a global platform devoted to spreading ideas in the form of short, impactful talks. Established in 1984, TED began as a conference bringing together thought leaders and innovators from diverse fields to share groundbreaking ideas. Today, it has evolved into a global phenomenon, oƯering talks that cover topics from science and technology to arts, culture, and social issues

. With the tagline “Ideas Worth Spreading,” TED inspires millions by making knowledge and insight accessible through TED Talks, events, and initiatives such as TED-Ed and TED Fellows. These talks are freely available online, breaking barriers and creating a world where knowledge knows no bounds. TED is more than a platform; it is a movement that fosters curiosity, innovation, and change, empowering individuals to shape a better tomorrow.

About TEDx

TEDx is an independently organized event, created in the spirit of TED’s mission to spread ideas and spark meaningful conversations. TEDx events are locally organized by passionate communities under a free license from TED, providing a space for unique perspectives and ideas to thrive. These events combine live speakers, recorded TED Talks, and engaging discussions to create a dynamic platform for intellectual exchange and inspiration. While TEDx shares TED’s commitment to high-quality storytelling and innovation, it is tailored to address the specific interests and challenges of the hosting community. Through TEDx, individuals are empowered to contribute to global dialogues on topics that matter, while also celebrating local talent and ideas. By fostering connections and nurturing creativity, TEDx events create a ripple eƯect, encouraging communities to imagine, innovate, and inspire collective action.
